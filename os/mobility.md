# Mobility

## What “mobility” means
Mobility = **active control** through a joint’s usable range of motion.
Not aggressive stretching. No pain.

## Neck CARs
CARs = Controlled Articular Rotations.
- 3–5 slow circles each direction
- 10–20 seconds per circle
- Shoulders stay down, no forcing range

## 10–15 minute evening reset
- Neck CARs
- Thoracic rotations (8–10/side)
- Hip flexor stretch (45s/side)
- 90-90 hips (6–8 controlled transitions)
- 2–3 min nasal breathing
